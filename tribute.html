Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

1630 - 1680

"जिंदगी में कुछ बनना है तो शेर की तरह जिओ, गीदड़ बनकर जिंदगी जीने से अच्छा है मर जाना।"

Biography

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India. Born on February 19, 1630 at Shivneri Fort, he is regarded as one of India's greatest warriors and an exemplary ruler.

Key achievements include:

  • Established Hindavi Swarajya (Self-rule of Hindu people)
  • Pioneered guerrilla warfare tactics
  • Built a strong naval force along the Konkan coast
  • Established efficient administration with Ashta Pradhan council
  • Promoted Marathi and Sanskrit languages

His military strategies and administrative innovations inspired future generations of leaders. Shivaji Maharaj's coronation in 1674 at Raigad Fort marked the formal establishment of the Maratha Empire.

Historical Legacy

Recognized as the Father of the Indian Navy for his naval innovations. His governance model emphasized religious tolerance and welfare of subjects.
